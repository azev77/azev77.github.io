Eigenvalues of general linear functions
=
Take a vector space $V$ over a field $F$. 
Let $T: V \to V$ be a linear function, that is
$T\left(\alpha v + \beta w \right) = \alpha T\left( v \right) + \beta T\left( w \right)$.
